##### 阿里什
# 埃及軍方宣稱在阿里什附近的空襲炸死超過45名伊斯蘭國西奈省成員，包括其首領安薩里。 

The Egyptian military says it has killed the head of the Sinai branch of so-called Islamic State, along with dozens of its fighters, in air strikes.
