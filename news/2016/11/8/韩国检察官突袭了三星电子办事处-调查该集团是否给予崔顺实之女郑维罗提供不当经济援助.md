# 韩国检察官突袭了三星电子办事处，调查该集团是否给予崔顺实之女郑维罗提供不当经济援助。 

South Korean prosecutors have raided the offices of Samsung Electronics as part of an investigation into the political scandal over President Park Geun-hye.
