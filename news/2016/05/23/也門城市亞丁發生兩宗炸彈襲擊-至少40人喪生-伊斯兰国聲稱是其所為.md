# 也門城市亞丁發生兩宗炸彈襲擊，至少40人喪生，伊斯兰国聲稱是其所為。 

At least 40 people, most of them army recruits, are killed in two bomb attacks claimed by so-called Islamic State (IS) in the Yemeni city of Aden.
