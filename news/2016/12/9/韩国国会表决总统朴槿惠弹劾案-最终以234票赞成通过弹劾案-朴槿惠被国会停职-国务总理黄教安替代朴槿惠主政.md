# 韩国国会表决总统朴槿惠弹劾案，最终以234票赞成通过弹劾案。朴槿惠被国会停职，国务总理黄教安替代朴槿惠主政。 

A slowing economy, a growing nuclear threat from North Korea and a more assertive China are some of the challenges facing the country.
