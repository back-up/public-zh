##### 古生物学家
#  古生物学家日前在美国犹他州发现蜥脚类恐龙的一个新物种阿比杜斯龙的化石。

Paleontologists have discovered a new dinosaur species that they've named Abydosaurus. The discovery includes the rare recovery of four sauropod skulls.
