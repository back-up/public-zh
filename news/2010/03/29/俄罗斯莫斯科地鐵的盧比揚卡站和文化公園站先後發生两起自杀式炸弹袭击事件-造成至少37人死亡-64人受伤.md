##### 俄罗斯
#  俄罗斯莫斯科地鐵的盧比揚卡站和文化公園站先後發生两起自杀式炸弹袭击事件，造成至少37人死亡、64人受伤。

Two explosions ripped through the busy Moscow metro on Monday morning during rush hour killing at least 34 people and wounding dozens more.
