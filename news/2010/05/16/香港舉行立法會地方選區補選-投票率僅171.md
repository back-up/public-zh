##### 香港
#  香港舉行立法會地方選區補選，投票率僅17.1%。

Most Hong Kong voters skipped a citywide legislative by-election Sunday, dealing a blow to opposition lawmakers who had hoped to use the polls as a de facto referendum on the pace of democratic reforms.
