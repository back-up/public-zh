#  尼日利亞政府軍在博爾諾州Konduga鎮擊退博科聖地武裝分子的進攻，約100名武裝分子被殺。 

The Nigerian army says it has killed some 100 Boko Haram militants after repelling an attack on a town near Maiduguri in north-eastern Borno state.
