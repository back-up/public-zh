##### 博科聖地
# 懷疑是博科聖地的武裝分子襲擊尼日利亞約貝州一間寄宿學校，至少29名學生被殺。 

Local people are angry that Nigerian forces withdrew security checkpoints before an attack on a school in Yobe state by Islamist militants.
