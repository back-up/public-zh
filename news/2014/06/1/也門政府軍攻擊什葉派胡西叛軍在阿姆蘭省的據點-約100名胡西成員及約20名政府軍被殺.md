#  也門政府軍攻擊什葉派胡西叛軍在阿姆蘭省的據點，約100名胡西成員及約20名政府軍被殺。 

At least 120 people were killed in the latest flare-up in the conflict between government forces and Zaidi Shia rebels in northern Yemen, reports say.
