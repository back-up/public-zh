# 二十國集團決定讓日本和沙烏地阿拉伯承辦2019年與2020年的二十國集團峰會 

Japan will host the G20 summit of leading nations in 2019, with Saudi Arabia presiding the 2020 edition, according to a statement Saturday winding up this year's event in Hamburg.
