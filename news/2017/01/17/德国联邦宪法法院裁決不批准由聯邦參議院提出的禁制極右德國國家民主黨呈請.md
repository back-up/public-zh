# 德国联邦宪法法院裁決不批准由聯邦參議院提出的禁制極右德國國家民主黨呈請。 

Federal judges in Germany have ruled against banning the far-right National Democratic Party (NPD). It is the second attempt to ban the NPD that has ended in failure.
