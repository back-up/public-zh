##### 美国财政部
# 美国财政部宣布对伊朗采取新的制裁，以应对伊朗最近的弹道导弹试验。 其中，13人和12个与伊朗有关系的企业受到影响。 

The U.S. Treasury Department has announced new sanctions against 13 people and 12 entities with ties to Iran, just days after Tehran conducted a new ballistic missile test.
