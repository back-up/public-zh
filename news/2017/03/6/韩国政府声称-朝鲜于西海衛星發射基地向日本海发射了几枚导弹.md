##### 韩国政府
# 韩国政府声称，朝鲜于西海衛星發射基地向日本海发射了几枚导弹 

The projectiles were launched Sunday from near North Korea's missile base of Tongchang-ri, says South Korea's military. At least one of the missiles flew about 620 miles.
