##### 伊朗里亞爾
# 伊朗里亞爾兌美元的黑市匯率再創新低，達到122,000里亞爾兌1美元。 

Iran's currency hit new lows with just less than a week before new U.S. economic sanctions are due to take effect on August 4, despite the appointment of a new central bank governor and a new economic team. Arab and Iranian media reported the Iranian rial hit a new low of 111,000 to the U.S.
