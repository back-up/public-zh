# 緬甸两名調查羅興亞穆斯林受迫害的路透社記者被當地法院判監7年 

A Myanmar court sentenced two Reuters journalists to seven years in prison for breaking the Official Secrets Act while investigating a massacre of Rohingya Muslims.
