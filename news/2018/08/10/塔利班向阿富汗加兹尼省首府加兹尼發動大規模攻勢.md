# 塔利班向阿富汗加兹尼省首府加兹尼發動大規模攻勢。 

Authorities in Afghanistan confirmed Monday that four days of fighting with the Taliban in and around the embattled southeastern city of Ghazni has killed 100 Afghan security personnel and 30 civilians. Defense Minister General Tariq Shah Bahrami says government forces, backed by allied airstrikes, have killed nearly 200 insurgents, including Arab, Chechen and Pakistani nationals.
