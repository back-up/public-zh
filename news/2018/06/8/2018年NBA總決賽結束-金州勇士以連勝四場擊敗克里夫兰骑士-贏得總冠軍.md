##### 2018年NBA總決賽
# 2018年NBA總決賽結束，金州勇士以連勝四場擊敗克里夫兰骑士，贏得總冠軍。 

Stephen Curry scores 37 points as the Golden State Warriors earn their third NBA title in four seasons with a 4-0 series win over the Cleveland Cavaliers.
