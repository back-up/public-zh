# 中國連續一周的暴雨導致南方多個省份遭受洪災，已知至少57人死，13人失蹤 

The death toll in China’s latest round of flooding has risen to at least 48, including two schoolchildren aboard an overloaded bus that plunged into a pond, authorities said Saturday.
