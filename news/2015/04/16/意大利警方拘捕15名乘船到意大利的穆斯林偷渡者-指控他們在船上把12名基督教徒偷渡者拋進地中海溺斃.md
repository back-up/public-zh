# 意大利警方拘捕15名乘船到意大利的穆斯林偷渡者，指控他們在船上把12名基督教徒偷渡者拋進地中海溺斃。 

Italian police say they have arrested 15 Muslim migrants after they allegedly threw 12 Christians overboard after a row on a boat heading to Italy.
