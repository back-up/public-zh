##### 尼日利亞
# 尼日利亞軍方發表聲明，表示在博爾諾州掃蕩了數個博科聖地營地，救出178名被擄平民。 

Nigeria's army says it has rescued 178 people, more than 100 of them children, from Boko Haram militants in the north-east of the country.
