# 疑似博科聖地武裝分子於6月30日及7月1日在尼日利亞博爾諾州襲擊了3條村，共殺死145名平民。 

Nearly 150 people are reported to have been killed by suspected Boko Haram Islamist militants in attacks in Nigeria's north-eastern Borno state.
