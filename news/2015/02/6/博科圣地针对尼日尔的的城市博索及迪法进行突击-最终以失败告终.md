##### 博科聖地
#  博科圣地针对尼日尔的的城市博索及迪法进行突击，最终以失败告终。 

Boko Haram launched its first major attack in Niger on Friday, triggering a forceful response from regional troops who claimed to have killed more than a hundred of the Islamists. The clashes in Bosso and Diffa, along the border with Nigeria, marked yet another expansion of violence attributed to Boko
