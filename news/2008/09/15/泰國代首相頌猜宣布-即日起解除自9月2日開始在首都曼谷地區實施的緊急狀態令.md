##### 泰国
# 泰國代首相頌猜宣布，即日起解除自9月2日開始在首都曼谷地區實施的緊急狀態令。

The acting prime minister of Thailand has lifted a state of emergency in the capital that was imposed on Sept. 2 amid an ongoing political crisis.
