# 巴基斯坦選舉委員會宣布，因前總理貝娜齊爾·布托遇刺身亡，國會選舉將由1月8日押後至2月18日。

The main opposition parties denounced the government’s decision on Wednesday to postpone parliamentary elections but said they would abide by the ruling.
