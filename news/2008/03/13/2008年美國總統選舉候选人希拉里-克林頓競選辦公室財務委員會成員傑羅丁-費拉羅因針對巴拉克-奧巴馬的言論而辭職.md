# 2008年美國總統選舉候选人希拉里·克林頓競選辦公室財務委員會成員傑羅丁·費拉羅因針對巴拉克·奧巴馬的言論而辭職。

In an interview, Geraldine A. Ferraro said that she stood by her controversial remarks, and accused the Obama campaign of deliberate distortion.
