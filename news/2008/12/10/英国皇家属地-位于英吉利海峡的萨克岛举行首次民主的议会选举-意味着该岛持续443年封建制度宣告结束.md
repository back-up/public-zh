##### 英国
# 英国皇家属地、位于英吉利海峡的萨克岛举行首次民主的议会选举，意味着该岛持续443年封建制度宣告结束。

The Channel island of Sark is waiting to discover the result of its first full parliamentary election after 443 years of feudal rule came to an end.
