##### 埃塞俄比亞航空302號班機空難
# 一架隸屬埃塞俄比亞航空的波音737MAX客機從埃塞俄比亞亚的斯亚贝巴博莱国际机场起飛後不久墜毀。 

An Ethiopian Airlines flight from Addis Ababa crashed shortly after takeoff Sunday morning, killing all 157 people onboard. The state-run Ethiopia Broadcasting Corporation said the flight that went down near the city of Bishoftu carried passengers from at least 33 countries.
