# 武裝分子襲擊索马里南部港口城市基斯马尤附近一個軍事基地，最終被擊退，至少70名武裝分子在該次進攻和隨後的空襲中喪生。 

U.S. and Somali military officials said Saturday that scores of al-Qaida-affiliated fighters had been killed during an attack on a government military base near Somalia's southern port city of Kismayo. The militants launched a surprise dawn attack Saturday at the base near Bula Gudud village, 50 kilometers north of Kismayo, starting with triple suicide blasts. Scores of heavily armed militants then stormed the base from all directions, Somali officials told VOA. "The terrorists have attacked the base with suicide blasts, and fierce fighting occurred.
