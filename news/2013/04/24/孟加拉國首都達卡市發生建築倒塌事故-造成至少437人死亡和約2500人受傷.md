##### 達卡市
#  孟加拉國首都達卡市發生建築倒塌事故，造成至少437人死亡和約2,500人受傷。

An eight-storey building housing garment factories collapses outside the Bangladeshi capital Dhaka, killing at least 87 people and injuring hundreds.
