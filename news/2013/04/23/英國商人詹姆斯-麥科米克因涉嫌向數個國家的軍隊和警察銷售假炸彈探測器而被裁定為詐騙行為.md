##### 假炸彈探測器
#  英國商人詹姆斯·麥科米克因涉嫌向數個國家的軍隊和警察銷售假炸彈探測器而被裁定為詐騙行為。

A millionaire businessman who sold fake bomb detectors to countries including Iraq and Georgia, knowing they did not work, is convicted of fraud.
