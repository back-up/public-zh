#  土耳其阿夫薩拉爾南南西方約80公里處發生規模5.8的地震 

A 5.8-magnitude earthquake struck Saturday in the Mediterranean Sea, some 50 miles (80 kilometers) south southwest of Avsallar, Turkey, the U.S. Geological Survey reported.
