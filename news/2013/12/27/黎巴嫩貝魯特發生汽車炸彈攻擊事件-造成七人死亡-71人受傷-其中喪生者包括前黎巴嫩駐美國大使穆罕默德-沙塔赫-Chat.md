##### 黎巴嫩
#  黎巴嫩貝魯特發生汽車炸彈攻擊事件，造成七人死亡，71人受傷。其中喪生者包括前黎巴嫩駐美國大使穆罕默德·沙塔赫 Chatah 

Mohamad Chatah, a former Lebanese minister of finance and ambassador to the United States, was killed Friday when a car bomb struck his convoy.
