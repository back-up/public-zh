#  美國消費者金融保護局以收取不正當費用和附加產品過多為由，命令美國運通必須支付罰款及賠償客戶7,500萬美元 

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau ordered American Express to pay more than $75 million to settle claims that it charged improper fees and misled its credit card customers over products such as identity-fraud protection.
