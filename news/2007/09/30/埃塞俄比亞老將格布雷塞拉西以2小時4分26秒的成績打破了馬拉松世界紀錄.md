# 埃塞俄比亞老將格布雷塞拉西以2小時4分26秒的成績打破了馬拉松世界紀錄。

Haile Gebrselassie shattered the world record in the marathon by 29 seconds, securing a mark he has coveted since he was a teenager in Ethiopia.
