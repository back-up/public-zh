# 法國發生槍擊案。一名男子在西南部城市圖盧茲一所猶太學校開槍打死三名學生和一名老師，事後逃脫。

Three children and a teacher are shot dead by a gunman at a Jewish school in the French city of Toulouse - it is being linked to shootings last week.
