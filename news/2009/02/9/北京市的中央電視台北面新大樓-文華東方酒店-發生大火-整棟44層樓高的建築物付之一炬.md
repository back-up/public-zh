# 北京市的中央電視台北面新大樓（文華東方酒店）發生大火，整棟44層樓高的建築物付之一炬。

A fierce blaze started by an illegal fireworks show engulfed one of the Chinese capital’s most architecturally celebrated modern buildings on Monday.
